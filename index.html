Welcome!

I am a fourth-year PhD student in the Department of Statistics at Harvard University, where I am advised by Professor Lucas Janson and Professor Jun Liu. My research interests include high-dimensional statistics, nonparametric statistics and Markov chain Monte Carlo.

Prior to Harvard, I obtained a B.S. in statistics from the Special Class for the Gifted Young, University of Science and Technology of China (Chinese: 中科大少年班) in 2016.
